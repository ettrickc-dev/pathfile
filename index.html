Questions, answered

Do I need a lawyer to use this?
No. Most people complete these forms themselves. We guide you through plain-English questions and prepare the forms for you. If your case is complex, you can add a licensed attorney review at checkout.
What if I make a mistake in my answers?
You review a free draft of every form before you pay, so you can check each answer. You can go back and edit anything, and your instructions show you exactly what to confirm before you mail.
Is my information secure?
Yes. Your information is used only to prepare your forms and deliver them to you. Sensitive fields like your Social Security number are optional — you can leave them blank and write them in by hand. Payments are processed securely by Stripe.
What happens after I pay?
Your completed package downloads right away and we email you a copy with a link to download it again anytime. Then you print, sign, attach your evidence, and mail it with payment — your instructions walk you through each step.
